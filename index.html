ServiceNow Interview Preparation

              
These are common architecture questions I often discuss when explaining CMDB, Discovery, and service visibility.

            


            

              
Question 1

              
How do you maintain CMDB data quality?

              
Answer

              
CMDB data quality depends on a combination of Discovery reliability, identification rules, reconciliation strategy, and normalization.

              
Key focus areas:

              
    
                
  • CI identification rules
    • 
                
  • reconciliation rules
    • 
                
  • normalization
    • 
                
  • CI ownership and lifecycle
    • 
              

            


            

              
Question 2

              
How do you troubleshoot Discovery failures?

              
Answer

              
Discovery troubleshooting typically follows this order:

              
    
                
  1. Network connectivity
    2. 
                
  2. Credentials
    3. 
                
  3. MID Server health
    4. 
                
  4. Discovery patterns
    5. 
                
  5. CI classification
    6. 
              

              
This approach isolates the layer where Discovery is failing.

            


            

              
Question 3

              
How do you implement CSDM successfully?

              
Answer

              
Successful CSDM implementation focuses on aligning the service model with how the organization delivers products.

              
This includes:

              
    
                
  • defining service boundaries
    • 
                
  • aligning CI classes to CSDM layers
    • 
                
  • connecting infrastructure to applications
    • 
                
  • linking services to business capabilities
