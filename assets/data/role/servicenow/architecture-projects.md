# Architecture Projects

## Enterprise CMDB Discovery Rollout

Organization: Dun & Bradstreet

Led the architecture and implementation of ServiceNow Discovery to establish a global CMDB inventory across the enterprise.

### Challenges

- Infrastructure visibility gaps
- Inconsistent asset inventory
- Operational silos across technology teams
- Architectural roadblocks preventing discovery coverage

### Solutions

- Established discovery architecture and governance framework
- Partnered with enterprise technology teams to resolve infrastructure gaps
- Implemented MID server infrastructure
- Defined discovery patterns and credential strategies
- Established governance processes for CMDB data quality

### Outcome

- Enabled automated discovery of enterprise infrastructure
- Established the first unified global CMDB inventory
- Improved service management and operational visibility
